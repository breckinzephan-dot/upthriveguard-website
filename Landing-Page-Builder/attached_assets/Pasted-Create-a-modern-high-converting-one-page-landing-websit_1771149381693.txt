Create a modern, high-converting one-page landing website for an Amazon reinstatement and suspension recovery service agency.

The website is focused on helping Amazon sellers who have suspended, deactivated, or restricted accounts recover their selling privileges through professional appeal strategy and account review.

Brand Name: The Upthrive Solution

Target Audience:
Amazon FBA and Seller Central account owners in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia whose accounts or listings have been suspended or deactivated.

Website Goal:
Convert visitors from TikTok Ads into WhatsApp consultations for a free account review.

Design Style:
Clean, professional, trustworthy, and premium agency style similar to a $10k/month ecommerce service agency. Use simple layouts, clear typography, and strong call-to-action buttons. Avoid clutter. Mobile-first design is very important.

Structure and Sections:

1. Hero Section (Above the Fold)
   Headline: “Amazon Account Suspended? Get Expert Reinstatement Help Fast”
   Subheadline: “We help Amazon sellers recover suspended and deactivated accounts with professional appeal strategies and structured reinstatement support.”
   Primary CTA Button: “Get Free Account Review on WhatsApp”
   Add urgency-focused supporting text such as “Don’t submit another wrong appeal.”

2. Problems We Solve Section
   Create a clean grid showing common Amazon suspension issues:

* Account Deactivation
* Section 3 Violations
* Inauthentic Item Complaints
* Related Account Suspension
* Listing or ASIN Suspension
* Appeal Rejected Cases
* Policy Violations
* Performance Issues

3. How Our Process Works
   Show a simple 3-step process:
   Step 1: Free Account Review
   Step 2: Appeal Strategy & Plan of Action Creation
   Step 3: Submission Guidance & Follow-Up

4. Why Choose Us Section
   Focus on trust and professionalism:

* Experienced with Amazon suspension cases
* Professional appeal writing
* Clear communication
* Fast response time
* Seller-focused solutions

Do not include unrealistic claims like “guaranteed reinstatement”.

5. Call-To-Action Section
   Headline: “Stop Losing Sales Due to Suspension”
   Button: “Chat on WhatsApp Now for Free Review”

6. FAQ Section
   Include common questions such as:

* How long does reinstatement take?
* What information do I need to start?
* Do you work with all marketplaces?
* What if my appeal was already rejected?

7. Footer
   Include brand name, contact email, WhatsApp contact button, and short disclaimer stating results depend on Amazon’s final decision.

Technical Requirements:

* Fully mobile optimized
* Fast loading
* Sticky WhatsApp button
* Designed for paid traffic conversion
* Simple navigation (single page scrolling)

Tone:
Professional, confident, solution-oriented, and trustworthy. Written for serious Amazon sellers who are losing revenue due to suspension.
